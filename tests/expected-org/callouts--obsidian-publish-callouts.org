To create a callout, add ~[!info]~ to the first line of a blockquote.

#+begin_src markdown
> [!info] A callout title
> Here is the callout **body** content.
#+end_src

#+begin_quote
[!info] A callout title
Here is the callout *body* content.
#+end_quote

*** Foldable callouts

A minus sign collapses the callout.

#+begin_src markdown
> [!faq]- Is this foldable?
> Yes, the content is hidden when collapsed.
#+end_src

#+begin_quote
[!question]- Is this foldable?
Yes, the content is hidden when collapsed.
#+end_quote

*** Supported types

Each type has a different color and icon.

#+begin_quote
[!note] Note
#+begin_src md
> [!note]
> Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet
#+end_src
#+end_quote

-----

#+begin_quote
[!abstract]- Abstract
#+begin_src md
> [!abstract]
> Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet
#+end_src
#+end_quote

Aliases: ~summary~, ~tldr~

-----

#+begin_quote
[!tip]- Tip
#+begin_src md
> [!tip]
> Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet
#+end_src
#+end_quote

Aliases: ~hint~, ~important~
